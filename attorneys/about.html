About LB Mokoena Attorneys

LB Mokoena Attorneys is a startup legal practice dedicated to providing clear, practical, and trustworthy legal advice. Our team brings together experience in commercial law, property, employment, and family law. We aim to be accessible and responsive to our clients—especially entrepreneurs and small businesses.

Our Approach

We emphasize clear scopes, transparent fees, and constant communication. Fixed-fee solutions are provided where possible, and we give realistic risk assessments for every matter. Your priorities are always our focus.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to simplify legal complexities for our clients, ensure fairness, and uphold integrity in every engagement. We value professionalism, responsiveness, and long-term client relationships.
